Welcome to the Vegetable Management System

• This is a simple system for managing vegetables database system designed and developed by Lionel Lwamba. You can use this app to easily keep track of your vegetable inventory.

• Add Vegetables: Click the "Add Vegetable" button to add a new vegetable to your inventory. Provide the vegetable's name, quantity, and price per unit. The app will automatically calculate the total value of the vegetable. Click the "Add" button to save the vegetable to your inventory.

• Query Vegetables: Use the "Query" button to search for specific vegetables by their name. Simply enter the name of the vegetable you're looking for and click "Query." The app will display a list of matching vegetables along with their quantity and price.

• Edit Vegetables: If you need to update the information of a vegetable, click the "Edit" link next to the vegetable in the inventory table. You can then modify the vegetable's name, quantity, and price and click "Save" to update it.

• Total Value: The app also provides a total sum of the values of all vegetables in your inventory. This can help you keep track of the total worth of your vegetable stock.

• Start managing your vegetables efficiently with this easy-to-use app!
